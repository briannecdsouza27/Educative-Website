About Us

Eduford College offers students better learning for broader impact as scholars, creators, citizens, and practitioners. Committed to critical inquiry and ethical practice in local and global settings, we foster an inclusive intellectual learning community to prepare students for lives of integrity, ambition, and purpose.


  • Our mission: We are a Christ-centered, student-focused community, developing diverse leaders of purpose, character, wisdom and transformational mindset, eager and equipped to make the world a better place.


  • Our vision: To be the leading Christ-centered university in the world, radically championing the pursuit of life abundant for all people.


  • Our values: Integrity, Inquiry, Collaboration, Service and Humility

Excellence in teaching and research

Eduford provides students the opportunity to engage with big ideas, to cross conceptual and disciplinary boundaries, and to become global citizens who embrace diversity of thought and experience.
